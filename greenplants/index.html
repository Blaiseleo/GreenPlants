ABOUT US
find out who we are.

Green-Plants is a Nigerian based agricultural company consisting of specialist products and service businesses. These businesses operates within five key markets and the various sectors that makes up each markets.

At Green-Plants, We offer a huge selection of plants , tools & equipments, natural herbal medicine, House Decoration Services and learning courses available Service Centers Nationwide. We invest multi-million pounds each year in new products to make sure that you hire reliable, safe and enviromentally friendly equipment incorporating the latest technology.

We also provide provide responsible, flexible and accessible quality services based on our core diagnostic capability, specialist sksills and the deployment of the latest innovative technology. From individual samples to large-scale surveys we operate to quality assured standard.
